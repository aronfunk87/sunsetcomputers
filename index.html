About Us

The Evolution of Sunset Computers... on the road to you

It all started with two CD-ROMs…

In 2000, Ed had the opportunity to start his own business. He was tech savvy, loved computers, and saw that they were becoming increasingly important in business and in life. With limited resources he started in an office in his home. Two CD ROM's were purchased and resold and Sunset Computers was born.

Within a few years he had moved to the spare bedroom, lined it with shelves to hold parts and ran cabling to support the three work stations. Son Aron followed in dad’s footsteps and joined the business as well.

2010 brought more growth and it was apparent we needed more space so plans were drawn up to build a 36 x 50 building adjacent to the garage. Finding the right style of workstations with high weight capacity and in the right size/configurations was a challenge, but Ed was not discouraged. This innovative entrepreneur pulled out his carpenter belt and his MIG welder, designed and built custom desks with laminated tops that would withstand tools and the challenges of the workday. We ended up selling a few of those desks as well!

Ed unfortunately passed away in 2023. Aron has been running the business since, continuing existing services, and expanding into providing Linux and other open-source solutions for local customers and companies.
